About BCSSL

Butterfly Conservation Society of Sri Lanka (ශ්‍රී ලංකා සමනල සංරක්ෂණ සංසදය) is the first organization founded in the country for the conservation of moths and butterflies. Our vision is to protect the biodiversity and the balance of natural environment through conservation of butterflies and moths. Our mission includes: conduct research and collect data on this faunal group, build inter-relationship between scientists and amateurs who study them, create awareness among the general public, assist the government in conservation efforts, encourage our own members to study them in depth.

We value the diversity of people in our organization since we believe that diversity is strength. Our members are diverse in profession, age and knowledge, yet we all have in common the endless love for these soft-winged beauties. Anybody who has a flair for them are always welcomed by the organization.
